Perspective

What I believe about building products

Agentic AI changes everything

We're going from copilots that help you work to agents that work on your behalf. Enterprise security won't be about staring at dashboards much longer. It'll be agents that detect threats, reason about context, and respond before a human could. I'm shipping the platform that lets partners build these.

Platforms win by making partners successful

Your own features aren't the moat. The ecosystem is. If partners can build, ship, and grow on your platform faster than anywhere else, you win. That's why I care so much about developer experience, clean APIs, and cutting the time it takes to go from idea to live product.

Ship first, then iterate on signal

SRE taught me that perfect is the enemy of uptime. I bring that same instinct to product: figure out the smallest thing that teaches you something real, ship it, measure what happens, and decide what to do next. Ambiguity doesn't scare me.
